1) Introduction / Кіріспе
This small Library Management System allows staff to add books and users, readers to search for books by title or author, and borrow/return books.
Бұл шағын жүйе қызметкерлерге кітаптар мен қолданушыларды қосуға, оқырмандарға іздеуге және кітаптарды алу/қайтаруға мүмкіндік береді.

2) Architecture Rationale / Архитектура негіздемесі
- Central Library class implemented as a Singleton manages books and users.
- Person is an abstract base class; Reader and Staff inherit from it.
- Search functionality uses Strategy pattern to separate search behaviors.
- UserFactory demonstrates a simple Factory pattern.

3) OOP Principles / ООП принциптері
- Encapsulation: private fields (prefixed with underscore) and properties.
- Inheritance: Reader and Staff inherit from Person.
- Polymorphism: SearchStrategy is an abstract interface implemented by concrete strategies.
- Abstraction: Person and SearchStrategy are abstract concepts.
